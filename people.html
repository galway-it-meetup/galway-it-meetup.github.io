A community is nothing without it's members. We are so thankful for all of you who come along and share your knowledge, experience and company.

Whether you are a regular, or have only come along once, we invite you to add your profile to this page. This is done through a Pull Request on Github, but don't worry, we have a whole guide showing you how to do this. Just click the big friendly button below!


Add your Profile

Why should you add your name and face to our meetup?

  • It's a great help for staying connected will felow members - If you met someone but in all the excitement didn't exchange details, you might find them here.
  • It's a novel online profile away from traditional social media.
  • Profiles are made via a github Pull Request - it's a way to learn about source control and make an Open Source Contribution!
  • We want to show off how many awesome people are part of our community.

For our October Meetup we've done something a little different - we partnered with the __[Galway Product Management Meetup](https://www.linkedin.com/company/product-management-meetup-galway/posts/)__ to host a discussion on __Open Source__!

The __[Galway Product Management Meetup](https://www.linkedin.com/company/product-management-meetup-galway/posts/)__ is managed by a collective of Galway Professionals under the umbrella of __[ITAG](https://itag.ie/)__ and they often host meetups similar to our own in the __PorterShed__!

To bring together our two communities, we decided on a discussion topic important to both Product & Engineering : Open Source. It may seem to a mostly technical topic, but we had an even split between product & engineering in the Q&A session which followed our two talks:

- __Intro & Open Source Tech__ by [Liam Krewer](https://www.linkedin.com/in/liamkrewer/)
- __Open Source Product Management__ by [Keerthi Chandra Sekaren](https://www.linkedin.com/in/keerthi-chandra-sekaren/)


Thank you to the PorterShed for sponsoring the event, and to the [Product Management Meetup Galway](https://www.linkedin.com/company/product-management-meetup-galway/posts/) for the collaboration - we hope to co-organize an event with you again in future!

Kicking off 2026 to a fantastic start with two excellent Cyber Security talks at __GIT 3.1: Cyber Security with Centripetal__.
We had a record of over 125 signups across Meetup & Eventrbrite and over 70 attendees. 

[Centripetal](https://www.centripetal.ai/)  specializes in Real Time Threat Prevention and have set up their newest location at [Platform94](https://platform94.com/) in Mervue. If you are looking for a Cyber Security Job in Galway, be sure to check out [Centripetal's Careers Page](https://www.centripetal.ai/company/careers).

We hosted two excellent talks by two returning speakers of GalwayIT:
- __Leasons Learnt from the Discord Data Breach__ by [Aisling Krewer](https://www.linkedin.com/in/aislingkrewer/)
- __AI for Cyber Security for 2026 and beyond__ by [Nithin Ravi](https://www.linkedin.com/in/nithinravi10/)

Thank you to Centripetal and The PorterShed for sponsoring the event!
